Reasons for Toxic Air

1. TRAP- Traffic Related Air Pollution

TRAP is a major source of air pollution in India, caused by slow moving traffic, which exacerbates pollution emissions due to heightened fuel consumption and diminished engine efficiency.

Researchers have found an 80 % increase in tailpipe emissions due to traffic congestion and that when vehicle speeds decline from 50 km/h to 20 km/h during congestion, emissions such as CO and CxHy can surge by approximately 50% due to partial burning of fuel.

Also, constant braking and friction with the road surface results in creation of NEEs (Non Exhaust Emissions) releasing PM 2.5 and PM 10 into the atmosphere containing brake dust, fine rubber particles, heavy metals and VOCs.
